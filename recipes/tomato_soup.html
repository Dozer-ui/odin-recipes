Tomato Soup

This is the real deal folks.

Tomato soup is a classic. However, there are people out there who have only ever had canned tomato soup! That's a crime, honestly. This recipe uses canned tomatoes but holy mackeral of gawd is it good! Better yet, if you're a tomato grower, substitute canned for your very own fresh garden tomatoes!

Ingredients

  • 1 (28 oz) can whole pureed tomatoes (or crushed run through a blender)

  • 2 cloves minced garlic

  • 2 tbs olive oil

  • 2 cups chicken broth

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute. DO NOT BURN YOUR GARLIC! It over-cooks pretty quickly.

  2. Add pureed tomatoes, chicken broth and salt and pepper to taste. Heat at medium-high. (If you used fresh tomatoes you can bring this up to a simmer for 10-15 minutes.)

  3. Once it's nice and hot, lower the heat to medium and add cream. Stir until fully incorporated.

  4. Bring to slight simmer, stirring constantly for about 10 minutes.

  5. Now whip up your best grilled cheese and chow down! Also very good with mozzerella sticks!

  6. Enjoy!
